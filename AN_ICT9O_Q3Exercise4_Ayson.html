Baguio City

Baguio City is a mountain resort city in Benguet, Northern Luzon, Philippines. Considered the countryâ€™s summer capital, Baguio is undoubtedly one of the top tourist destinations in the Philippines. Also nicknamed The City of Pines because Baguio is full of pine trees, Baguio is a popular weekend getaway for local travelers based in the metro because of its cool climate, foggy hills, panoramic views, and lovely flowers. Baguio is also known for its strawberries, ube, and all pasalubong foods and things.

Why is Baguio City Special To Me?

Baguio city is one of my favorite childhood places to go, and until now because since I was a child, Baguio has been the place where we always stayed to have fun and relax. After how many years, going to Baguio is typical for us, as we visit Baguio once or twice a month. And going to Baguio during Christmas was also our tradition.

Hotels or Places in Baguio We Have Stayed In

As a person who is always going to Baguio, we only stayed in 3 different hotels, which are the Manor Hotel at Camp John Hay, the White House which is also in Camp John Hay, and the Alphaland Baguio Mountain Lodges, and I really recommend these three places to stay.

Things to do in Baguio and Foods to Taste!

In Baguio city, there are many things to do and places to go to, such as The Igorot Stone Kingdom, Tam-awan Village, Tree Top Adventure, Picnic at Camp John Hay, Baguio Night Market, Burnham Park, Sky Ranch Baguio, Mines View Park, Horseback Riding in Camp John Hay, Good Shepherd Products, Baguio Botanical Garden, Wright Parkand in Baguio, not only tourist places are popular, but there are also culinary attractions such as Ube and Strawberry Taho, Strawberry Ice Cream, Benguet Coffee and Ube Jam; to be honest, there are a lot more places to go and foods to taste, and I assure you that Baguio is a very beautiful place.
